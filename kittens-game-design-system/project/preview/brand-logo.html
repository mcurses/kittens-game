BRAND THEMES

— what we are, what we are not —
In a grim and dark future of catkind,
no one can hear you scream.

GOOD

lean into
  • Mythical monsters leviathans, elder gods, things older than the catnip forest
  • Elder artifacts blueprints, time crystals, relics of dead empires
  • Arcane technologies chronosphere, antimatter, hand-of-god rituals
  • Lost civilizations ruins, prophecies, things kittenkind has forgotten

BAD

never · ever
  • NO ROBOTS not androids, not mechs, not "friendly AI". we have arcane machines, not silicon valley pals.
  • Elves too tidy. wrong genre.
  • Fairies cute > mythic. drains the dread.
  • Owls another forest's mascot. not ours.
Voice rule. Bone-dry, observational, slightly fatalistic. Kittens starve, kittens die, the universe is indifferent. Never apologize, never console. The player chose this.
