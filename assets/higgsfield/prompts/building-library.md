# building-library

**Subject**: A small wooden library interior with shelves of scrolls and books, 1-2 chibi pixel-art cats reading or perched on a stack of tomes, a single candle or lantern providing soft light

**Style anchors**: pixel art, Stardew-Valley-warmth, cozy reading nook, hard pixel edges, no aliased outlines

**Palette anchors**:
- dominant: `--res-wood #8B5A2B` (shelves)
- secondary: `--res-science #7A9DC2` (scroll/book accents — subtle, not loud)
- background: `--bg #F2E6CF` (parchment) or one tone darker for interior depth
- ink accent: `--ink #2B221A` (cat outlines, shadow lines)

**Negative**: photorealistic, 3D, anime, blurry, watermark, text on book spines, numbers, UI, modern app icon, glassmorphism, bloom, neon, anti-aliased outlines, magic glow effects

**Aspect**: 1:1, 1024×1024 raw → downsampled to 256×256 final WEBP export

**Belongs to loop beats**: `curiosity`, `specialization` (see `agent-docs/GAME_LOOP.md`) — should feel contemplative, slightly mysterious, but warm.
