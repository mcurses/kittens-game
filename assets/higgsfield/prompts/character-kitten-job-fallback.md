# character-kitten-job-fallback

**Status**: planned — 8 generic per-job portraits used when a kitten has no unique portrait yet.
**Aspect**: 1:1, 1024×1024 raw → 512×512 export (standard NN-pixelation pass).
**Pipeline**: same as `character-kitten-composer.md` (cwebp lossless, with the standard pixelation pass).

These 8 portraits exist so every Census-card and Inspector slot has something to display while individual kitten portraits are still being generated. The composer-engineered "average kitten of this job" — neutral breed (tabby), average body, friendly eyes, no accessory, full job outfit.

After generation, place at `assets/exports/characters/job-{name}.webp`.
